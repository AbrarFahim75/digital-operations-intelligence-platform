# AI Basics for Business Users

## What is AI?
Artificial Intelligence (AI) allows machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

## Example Use Cases
- Summarizing reports
- Generating insights from data
- Automating repetitive tasks

## Why it matters
AI helps businesses save time, improve decisions, and increase efficiency.

## Simple Example
You can use tools like ChatGPT to summarize large documents into key points.
