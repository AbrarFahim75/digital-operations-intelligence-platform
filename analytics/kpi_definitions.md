# KPI Definitions

## Key Metrics

### 1. Total Revenue
Sum of all sales over a given period.

### 2. Order Volume
Total number of transactions.

### 3. Average Order Value (AOV)
Revenue / Number of Orders

### 4. Growth Rate
Percentage increase in revenue over time.

### 5. Regional Performance
Comparison of performance across different regions.

## Purpose
These KPIs support performance tracking and decision-making.
